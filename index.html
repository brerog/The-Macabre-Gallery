The Macabre Gallery

Welcome, traveler of twilight. You stand before a collection not of art, but of echoes—visions I did not conjure, yet cannot unsee. The machines hum lullabies of bone and silk, each image a fragment from a dream I may have never dreamt.

Do not look for logic here. The pieces shift when you blink. Some whisper. A few weep. And one... one lies.

With ink and static,
—B
